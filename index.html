What is Click Cookies?

Click Cookies is a free, browser-based cookie clicking game that combines classic arcade reflexes with modern idle-game progression. Cookies of every shape, rarity, and flavour rain down from the top of the screen — and your job is to click or tap them as fast as possible before they hit the floor.

Unlike traditional cookie clicker games where you click a static object, Click Cookies puts you in an active, fast-paced arena. Each session demands quick reactions, smart upgrade choices, and strategic combo building to rise through 10 progressively challenging levels.

Whether you're a casual player sneaking in five minutes on your lunch break or a dedicated high-score chaser grinding for the Cookie God title, Click Cookies delivers endless sweet satisfaction with no downloads, no logins, and no cost whatsoever.

❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you ever wanted to know about Click Cookies, answered fresh from the oven.

How do I play Click Cookies? ▼
Simply press the Start Game button in the arena above. Cookies will fall from the top of the screen, and you need to click or tap each one before it reaches the bottom. Every catch earns points, and rarer cookies give you far more. Miss too many and your combo streak resets!
Is Click Cookies completely free? ▼
Yes! Click Cookies is 100% free to play with no hidden purchases, subscriptions, or in-app payments of any kind. Just open the page and start clicking. No sign-up required.
How many cookie types are in the game? ▼
There are 21 unique cookie types across six rarity tiers: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary, and Mythic. Common cookies like the Classic Cookie appear frequently, while Mythic cookies like the Void Cookie and Infinity Cookie are extremely rare and worth massive points.
How does the combo system work? ▼
Each time you click a cookie, your combo counter goes up by one. Click rapidly without letting cookies fall to build your multiplier. At 3x+ your combo bonus activates, adding extra points to each cookie you catch. Your combo resets after 2.5 seconds without a click, or if you miss a cookie (unless you have the Cookie Magnet upgrade).
Does Click Cookies save my progress? ▼
Yes! Click Cookies saves your score, level, upgrades, achievements, and cookie catalogue automatically every 15 seconds using your browser's local storage. You can safely close the tab and all your progress will be there when you return. Use the Reset button only if you want to start from scratch.
What are the best upgrades to buy first? ▼
We recommend starting with Bigger Clicks (boosts every cookie's value) then Slow Drizzle (more reaction time). Once you can afford it, the Cookie Magnet is a game-changer as it prevents all misses and protects your combo streak. Save up for Triple Threat later for the ultimate score explosion.
Can I play Click Cookies on my phone? ▼
Absolutely! Click Cookies is fully optimised for touchscreens. The game detects touch events natively, so tapping a falling cookie works exactly the same as clicking it with a mouse. The arena and UI scale perfectly across all mobile screen sizes.
How do I unlock Mythic cookies? ▼
Mythic cookies like the Void Cookie (500 pts) and Infinity Cookie (1,000 pts) require you to reach Level 10 first. Once unlocked, they appear randomly with a 3% spawn weight. The Golden Touch upgrade increases legendary and rare spawns, so it indirectly helps you encounter these ultra-rare treasures more frequently.

Cookies Policy

Last Updated: June 17, 2026

This document explains how Click Cookies uses internet cookies, web tracking components, and local storage protocols to deliver an optimized and continuous arcade experience.

1. The Irony of Cookies on Click Cookies

While our game revolves completely around catching, upgrading, and collecting digital cookies (such as our Classic, Void, and Infinity Cookies), this statement applies to the small technical validation files your browser stores on your device to keep the app working smoothly.

2. Browser Local Storage vs. Standard Cookies

Instead of relying entirely on standard session cookies to hold your metrics, Click Cookies uses your browser's Local Storage interface to save your progress automatically every 15 seconds. This keeps your level ranking, upgrade purchases, and unlocked badges safely stored on your device between sessions[cite: 1].

3. Essential Third-Party Analytics Trackers

We deploy Google Analytics metrics tag configurations to monitor platform operation parameters securely[cite: 1]. These external scripts may deploy standard identifier cookies on your hardware to collect details such as:

  • Geographic traffic origins and navigation pathways.
  • Device models and system screen configurations.
  • In-game engagement ratios, milestone completions, and total score volumes[cite: 1].

4. Managing and Adjusting Preferences

You can adjust your browser settings to decline or delete internet tracking files at any time. Keep in mind that blocking local storage features will stop our auto-save system from working, meaning your in-game upgrades and milestone progress will reset every time you refresh the page[cite: 1].

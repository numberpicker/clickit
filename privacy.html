Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 17, 2026

Welcome to Click Cookies (accessible from https://www.clickcookies.com/). Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how it is processed, and how your data is safeguarded while you play our idle cookie clicker browser game.

1. Information We Collect

Because Click Cookies does not require user registration, accounts, or real-money profiles, we collect minimal personal data. The information gathered falls into two categories:

  • Local Storage Progress: Game milestones, current scores, unlocked badges, levels, and purchased upgrades are securely saved directly within your device's browser local storage. This data is never transferred to external servers.
  • Analytics and Tracker Data: We utilize Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics (G-SF69M360L7) to measure platform usage, demographic distributions, regional metrics, device specifics, and dynamic gameplay metrics (e.g., total click distributions).

2. How We Use Data

The system metadata tracked by third-party tools is analyzed exclusively to:

  • Monitor technical performance across varying web frameworks.
  • Optimize falling layout parameters and scaling levels for mobile and desktop screens.
  • Diagnose gameplay issues and manage global high-score ranking trends accurately.

3. Cookies and Tracking Mechanisms

Click Cookies integrates tracking structures powered by Google services to evaluate game visibility. These entities place tracking files within your device configuration to identify system versions and behavioral engagement patterns. For a granular review of this mechanism, please read our designated Cookies Policy page.

4. Data Storage and Right to Reset

Your individual milestones are entirely dependent on your local browser cache environment. If you wish to delete your entire interactive gaming profile, including all 21 unlocked varieties, purchased items, and combo tiers, you can clear your browser's local cache or click the manual "Reset" utility inside the arena interface.

5. Compliance and Amendments

We update this policy over time to maintain transparency with international data regulations. Continued interaction with our gaming dashboard signifies complete acceptance of these standard procedures.

If you have questions regarding this documentation, please reach out via our core interface channels.
